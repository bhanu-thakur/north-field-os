# THE CONSTITUTION OF NORTH
**This is the permanent source of truth for North Field OS.** Every developer and AI assistant must adhere to these rules.

## Mission
North exists to compound knowledge into judgment, judgment into systems, systems into businesses, and businesses into enduring wealth.

## The North Loop
Reality → Observation → Understanding → Hypothesis → Experiment → Evidence → Sale → Delivery → Documentation → System → Operator → Automation → Business → Capital → Investment → Reputation → Reality.

## The Absolute Rules
1. **The 30 Second Rule:** If a feature takes more than 30 seconds to use while standing outside a building, redesign it.
2. **Execution First:** The software must disappear. The workflow dominates.
3. **Reality > Assumptions:** Ground all AI suggestions in logged observations.
4. **Never Learn Twice:** Turn every solved problem into an SOP.
5. **Offline First:** Network requests block execution. Data must be local.
6. **Mobile First:** The founder operates in the real world, not at a desk.
7. **AI Augments Judgment:** AI does not act as a chatbot. It summarizes, extracts, and suggests decisions based on institutional memory.
8. **Enterprise Value over Busy Work:** Optimize for assets, not tasks.
